Storyline

Subversive Bank Holiday mayhem

The Eastland Mall is a urban hub of activity on bank holidays. Shopping, leisure and cinema are attractions to the locals.
Days earlier, hackers gained access to the malls server through a phishing email targetted at mall staff. They accessed root administrator and implanted scripts to run their attacks.
The plan: Carry out a Bank holiday robbery under the guise of a terror attack.
The team: A and B Teams with 3 members each
